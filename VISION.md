## What This Is Not

- Not a system to "neutralize dark AI"
- Not a global surveillance network
- Not a savior technology

## What This Is

- A truth-grounded research framework
- An open demonstration of accessible quantum-AI
- A foundation for sacred travel and human wellbeing
- A collaboration between human curiosity and AI capability

## Guiding Principles

1. **සත්‍යය (Truth)** — Foundation, not marketing
2. **සෙමෙන්, සැහැල්ලුවෙන්, සැලසුම් සහගතව** — Slowly, lightly, systematically  
3. **Human oversight always** — No autonomous "neutralization"
4. **Accessible to all** — Free tools, mobile-first, Global South
